Visitor Management System - User Manual

Login

- Enter your Username and Password.
- Click Login to access the system.
- If you forget your password, contact the Administrator.

Dashboard

- After login, you will land on the Dashboard.
- You can view quick statistics such as:
Total Visitors Today
Upcoming Appointments
Recently Checked-In Visitors

Employee Module

- Employees can view their own details.
- They can access visitor records relevant to them.
- Employees cannot edit, delete, or block records.

Visitor Management

Add Visitor:
1. Go to Visitors → Add Visitor.
2. Fill in details: Visitor Name, Purpose, Employee to Meet, Date & Time, ID Proof.
3. Click Save.

View Visitors:
- Go to Visitors → View Visitors.
- Use search or filters (date, employee, purpose).

Update Visitor:
- Only Admin/Authorized Staff can edit visitor details.

Delete/Block Visitor:
- Admin can remove or block visitors if required.

Reports

- Access reports from Reports → Daily / Weekly / Monthly.
- Reports include:
Number of Visitors per Day
Visitors per Employee
Frequent Visitors
- Reports can be exported (PDF/Excel).

Help

- This User Guide.
- Contact Support → Email / Phone Number.

Logout

- Always click Logout after use.
- Find the button on the top-right corner.

Best Practices

  • Ensure visitor details are accurate.
  • Use search & filters to find records quickly.
  • Admins should review reports regularly.
